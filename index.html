Step-by-Step Guide

Setting Up Your Printer the Right Way

Follow these steps for a smooth, error-free printer setup. If you get stuck at any point, call us and we will guide you through it live.

1
Unbox and Position Remove all packaging tape and protective materials. Place your printer on a flat, stable surface near your router for the strongest wireless signal possible.
2
Install Ink or Toner Install ink cartridges or toner following your model's specific instructions. Always use the correct cartridge for your exact printer model to avoid errors.
3
Connect to WiFi Use the printer's control panel to connect to your home or office WiFi network. Keep your network name and password handy before you begin this step.
4
Install the Driver Download the correct driver for your printer model from the manufacturer's official website and install it on your computer following the on-screen prompts.
5
Print a Test Page Print a test page to confirm everything is working correctly. If the test page looks good, your setup is complete and you are ready to print.

Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions we receive. Still not sure? Call us — our specialists love answering questions.

Why is my printer showing offline when it is clearly turned on?
This is one of the most common printer issues and is almost always caused by a communication breakdown between your computer and printer. The fix typically involves restarting the print spooler service, removing and re-adding the printer in your settings, or resolving an IP address conflict on your network. Call us and we will walk you through the exact fix for your model in minutes.
Is your guidance call really free?
Yes, completely free. Our initial guidance call costs nothing. We will diagnose your problem and walk you through the solution at no charge. There are no hidden fees, no subscription required, and no obligation to purchase anything. We simply want to help you get your printer working again.
Can you help with any printer brand or model?
Absolutely. Our specialists are trained and certified on all major printer brands including HP, Canon, Epson, Brother, Xerox, Lexmark, Ricoh, Samsung, and more. Whether your printer is brand new or several years old, we have seen your model before and know how to help you.
How long does a typical printer problem take to resolve?
Most common printer problems — including offline errors, driver issues, paper jams, connectivity problems, and print quality issues — are fully resolved within 15 to 30 minutes on a single call. We resolve 97% of problems on the very first call without any need for follow-up or in-person visits.
Do you provide help with setting up a brand new printer?
Yes, absolutely. New printer setup is one of our most popular services. Our specialists will walk you through the entire process from unboxing to your first successful print page — including ink installation, WiFi connection, driver setup, and any software configuration your specific model requires.
What if my printer issue cannot be fixed by phone?
In the rare case that a problem cannot be resolved remotely — for example, if there is a physical hardware failure — our specialists will clearly explain the situation, tell you exactly what is wrong, and help you decide on the best next step, whether that is a warranty claim, a local repair, or a replacement recommendation that fits your budget.
